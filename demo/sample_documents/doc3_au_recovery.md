# Gold and Silver Recovery from Refractory Ores

## Process Description
Recovery of Au/Ag from refractory ores often requires pre-treatment via pressure oxidation (POX) or bio-leaching to break down sulfide matrices.

## Operating Conditions
Pressure oxidation typically operates at 210°C and 30 bar. The residence time in the autoclave is approximately 60 to 90 minutes.

## Results
This method increases gold recovery from 60% (direct cyanidation) to over 94%.
