# Water Desalination in Mining Operations

## Introduction
Mining sites often face high salinity in process water, requiring advanced desalination techniques to prevent equipment corrosion and improve metallurgical recovery.

## Methods
Reverse Osmosis (RO) is the most common method. For highly concentrated brines, Multi-Stage Flash (MSF) distillation is preferred.

## Performance Indicators
The energy consumption for RO typically ranges from 3 to 5 kWh/m^3 depending on the feedwater salinity.
